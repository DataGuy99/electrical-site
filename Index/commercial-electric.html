Complete Commercial Electrical Solutions

From office buildings to retail spaces, warehouses to restaurants, G&C Electric provides comprehensive electrical services for commercial properties throughout East Tennessee. Our experienced team delivers on-time, on-budget solutions that keep your business running smoothly.

New Construction

Complete electrical design and installation for new commercial buildings. We work closely with contractors and architects to deliver efficient, future-proof electrical systems.

Tenant Improvements

Electrical upgrades and modifications for new tenants. We ensure your space meets all code requirements and business needs with minimal disruption.

Service & Maintenance

Regular maintenance programs and emergency repairs to keep your business operational. 24/7 emergency response available for critical issues.

Power Distribution

Panel upgrades, circuit additions, and power distribution optimization. We ensure your electrical infrastructure can handle current and future demands.

Data & Communications

Structured cabling, network infrastructure, and telecommunications systems. Modern businesses need reliable data infrastructure - we deliver.

Energy Efficiency

LED retrofits, occupancy sensors, and energy management systems. Reduce your energy costs while improving lighting quality and environmental impact.
