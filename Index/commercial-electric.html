Our Commercial Electrical Process

01

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to understand your project requirements, timeline, and budget. Site visits help us assess existing conditions and identify potential challenges early.

02

Design & Planning

Our team develops detailed electrical plans, load calculations, and equipment specifications. We coordinate with architects, contractors, and engineers to ensure seamless integration.

03

Permitting & Approvals

We handle all permit applications, code reviews, and utility coordination. Our experience with local jurisdictions ensures smooth approval processes.

04

Professional Installation

Certified electricians execute the work with precision and attention to detail. We maintain clean job sites, coordinate with other trades, and keep projects on schedule.

05

Testing & Inspection

Comprehensive testing ensures all systems function correctly and safely. We coordinate final inspections and address any items promptly for quick approval.

06

Project Closeout

Complete documentation, as-built drawings, warranty information, and system training. We remain available for any questions or follow-up needs.
