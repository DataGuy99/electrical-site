Our Panel Upgrade Process

01

Comprehensive Assessment

We evaluate your current panel, electrical load, and future needs. Load calculations determine proper sizing while code review identifies any compliance issues.

02

Detailed Planning

Coordination with utility company for service upgrades if needed. Permit applications, material procurement, and scheduling to minimize business downtime.

03

Safe Installation

Professional installation following manufacturer specifications and NEC requirements. Work performed by licensed electricians with proper safety protocols and equipment.

04

Testing & Verification

Comprehensive testing of all circuits, proper grounding, correct phasing, and breaker operation. Thermal imaging identifies any hot spots or connection issues.

05

Inspection & Approval

Final inspection by local authority having jurisdiction. We address any inspector comments promptly to ensure quick approval and project completion.

06

Documentation & Training

Complete panel schedules, circuit directories, and as-built documentation. Brief training on panel operation, breaker identification, and emergency procedures.
