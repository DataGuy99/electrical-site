LED Retrofit Process

01

Lighting Audit

We document existing fixtures, measure light levels, and identify opportunities for improvement. Our audit reveals potential energy savings and performance upgrades.

02

Energy Analysis

Detailed cost-benefit analysis showing current energy consumption, projected savings, and payback period. We help identify available utility rebates and incentives.

03

Design Proposal

Customized lighting plan with fixture selections, layout recommendations, and control options. We balance performance, aesthetics, and budget considerations.

04

Scheduled Installation

Work scheduled during off-hours to minimize business disruption. Professional installation with quality fixtures and proper disposal of old equipment.

05

System Commissioning

Fine-tune light levels, program controls, and verify all systems function correctly. Training provided for any automated or smart lighting features.

06

Performance Verification

Post-installation energy monitoring confirms projected savings. Warranty documentation and ongoing support for long-term satisfaction.
