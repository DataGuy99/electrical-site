Our Story

Founded in 2024, G&C Electric has quickly established itself as East Tennessee's premier electrical contractor. Our team brings together decades of combined experience in commercial and industrial electrical systems, with a commitment to delivering exceptional quality and unmatched customer service.

We understand that electrical work is the backbone of modern business operations. That's why we approach every project with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that your electrical systems are not just functional, but optimized for efficiency, safety, and future growth.

From ground-up commercial construction to complex industrial installations, from emergency repairs to preventive maintenance programs, G&C Electric is your trusted partner for all electrical needs. We pride ourselves on completing projects on time, within budget, and above expectations.
